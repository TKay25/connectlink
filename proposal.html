Executive Summary

Unified Messenger is a powerful, centralized communication platform that enables your business to manage customer conversations across WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Twitter/X, and Instagram DM — all from a single, intuitive dashboard. Streamline customer support, execute bulk marketing campaigns, maintain compliance with opt-in consent, and gain analytics insights without switching between apps.

With real-time messaging, contact database management, broadcast automation, and platform status monitoring, Unified Messenger helps you reduce response times, increase engagement, and drive more revenue through personalized omnichannel outreach.

Omnichannel Inbox

Manage all social messaging apps from one place. No more logging into multiple platforms.

Bulk Broadcast

Send targeted campaigns to opted-in contacts. Filter by tags, activity, or platform.

GDPR & Opt-in Ready

Automatic consent tracking, compliance-first contact storage for safe broadcasting.
