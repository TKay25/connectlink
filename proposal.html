📱 What is this system?

Bulk Messaging System is a software dashboard that lets you send mass messages to people who have contacted your resort through WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Instagram DM, Twitter/X, and LinkedIn — all from one place.

✅ No more copying/pasting messages to each customer.
✅ No more logging into five different apps.
✅ Just write your message once, choose who gets it, and click send.

🎯 What this Bulk Messenger will do for your resort
1. Send ONE message to ALL

Write an offer or news once. Select all contacts (WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn). The system sends it to everyone instantly.

2. Reach every single person who contacted you

Anyone who ever messaged your resort on any social platform is saved. Broadcast promotions, event updates, and offers to ALL past & potential customers.

3. INCLUDING LinkedIn for B2B

Send corporate package deals, conference invites, and partnership opportunities directly to LinkedIn connections who messaged your business page.

💬 Which apps are connected? (5 Platforms)
WhatsApp Messenger Instagram DM Twitter/X DM LinkedIn
  • WhatsApp: Everyone who messages your WhatsApp Business number
  • Facebook Messenger: Everyone who messages your resort Facebook page
  • Instagram DM: Everyone who DMs your resort Instagram account
  • Twitter/X DM: Everyone who sends a direct message to your Twitter handle
  • LinkedIn: Everyone who messages your company LinkedIn page (great for corporate clients & event planners)
Important: Messages are sent to ALL social media users who have contacted your business — including past guests, potential customers with inquiries, LinkedIn business partners, and event planners. The system respects opt-in rules and platform policies automatically.
