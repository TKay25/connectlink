🎯 What ConnectLink Is

ConnectLink is a comprehensive business management platform designed for construction/building companies and hardware retailers. It integrates project management, financial tracking, AI-powered inventory, and WhatsApp communication into one unified system.

📦 Core Modules & Features

1. Building Projects Module
✅ Project creation & management | ✅ Quotation generation | ✅ Automatic contract generation | ✅ Payment plans (deposits + 10 installments) | ✅ Work schedule planning with Gantt charts
2. Hardware & POS Module
✅ 25+ product categories | ✅ AI-powered classification (98% accurate) | ✅ Real-time inventory tracking | ✅ Sales analytics | ✅ Stock level alerts
3. Financial Management
✅ Payment tracking (deposits & installments) | ✅ Late fee calculation | ✅ Profitability analysis | ✅ Payment reminders | ✅ Financial reports
4. Client Management

💾 How Data Is Saved

Database Technology: PostgreSQL (Relational Database)

Core Tables:
• connectlinkdatabase - Projects, clients, contracts, payments
• quotations - Project cost estimates & line items
• project_schedules - Work timelines & Gantt chart data
• inventory - Hardware products & stock levels
• pos_transactions - Sales records
• installments - Monthly payment tracking
• users - Admin & staff accounts
• payments_outbox - WhatsApp message queue
• enquiries - Client inquiries & leads
